First US-RSE Workshop

Thanks to a generous grant from the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation, we held a first in person US-RSE Association community building workshop, in Princeton, NJ April 26-27, 2022. 50 attendees focused on planning the best path forward to grow the US-RSE Association.


Discussions are continuing in US-RSE slack. Join to get involved!
